My Recommendations

Here you'll find recommendations for things to do, the best restaurants to try, and insider tips!

Madrid, Spain

Things to do: Visit the Royal Palace, stroll through Retiro Park, eat your way through Mercado de San Miguel, and marvel at the art in the Prado Museum

Restaurants: Juana la Loca, Rosi la Loca, Ten Con Ten, and Amazonico

Tips!: Trying churros from San Gines is a must! San Gines is Madrid's oldest "chocolater√≠a" and stays true to its traditional roots. Although some say the skinny churros are better, I disagree and urge anyone visiting San Gines to order the thicker ones!

Santorini, Greece

Things to do: Attend a wine tasting, take a boat ride around the island, stroll through the village of Oia, and take in the incredible views from anywhere on the island

Restaurants: Argo, Metaxi Mas, and Cava Alta

Tips!: Make sure you stay in the village of Oia to see the sunset. Though you will probably be surrounded by many other tourists, it is something that can't be missed.

London, United Kingdom

Things to do: Take a ride on the London Eye, walk across Tower and London Bridges, watch the Changing of the Guards, and hop on a double-decker bus!

Restaurants: Fish!, Borough Market (not a restaurant, but definitely a must-try lunch spot!), Granger and Co., and Sketch (for the most amazing and aesthetically-pleasing afternoon tea)

Tips!: If you are a Harry Potter fan, the Warner Bros. Studio Tour is a must. This is a behind-the-scenes look at where the movies were filmed. Visitors can try butter beer, see the actual costumes worn by our favorite wizards, and even fly on a broomstick!

Florence, Italy

Things to do: Climb to the top of the Duomo, see the statue of David, walk across the Ponte Vecchio bridge, and try gelato!

Restaurants: La Giostra, Trattoria Gobbi 13, All'Antico Vinaio, and Il Profeta

Tips!: After a long day of touring around Florence, a relaxing evening is the perfect solution. Grab some snacks and drinks and head up to Piazzale Michelangelo. Though is this definitely a hike, the views make it all worth it, especially at sunset.

Interlaken, Switzerland

Things to do: Ski through the Jungfrau ski region, stroll through the quaint streets, and ride ATVs through the mountains

Restaurants: Ox Restaurant and Grill, Restaurant Aare, and Ladelokal

Tips!: A must-do in the quaint town of Interlaken is making your veru own swiss chocolate at the Funky Chocolate Club!. Funky Chocolate Club also has chocolate covered strawberries, the best hot chocolate, and a variety of other chocolate treats to indulge in!
