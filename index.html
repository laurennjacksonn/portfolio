About

Welcome! I have always loved learning about new cultures, trying new dishes, and everything else regarding exploring new parts of the world. This website will help me document all of my travels, past, present, and future! Included in this site is a "Where Have I Been?" page which documents every country I've travelled to, a Recommendations page, and a gallery of some of my favorite pictures I've taken. Currently, I am a student at the School of Information at the University of Michigan. In the Spring of 2019, I had the opportunity to study abroad! I studied in Madrid, Spain at the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid. Studying in Madrid gave me the opportunity to travel around Europe throughout the semester. My previous travelling experience had either been with family or on organized trips for teens. So far I have travelled to 37 countries and counting!
